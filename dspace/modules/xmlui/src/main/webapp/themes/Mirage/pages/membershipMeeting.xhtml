Previous Meetings

24 May, 2013, St Anne's College, Oxford, UK

The Dryad Membership Meeting was part of a series of separate but related events spotlighting trends in scholarly communication and research data.

The Now and Future of Data Publishing, a symposium, 22 May 2013

The symposium provided an overview of the current landscape, interrogated the apparent benefits for researchers and research more generally and examined visions of the future of data publication. Above all, what sort of data publication will most engage researchers and most benefit research? The day featured keynote presentations and panel sessions addressing key themes around data publication.

Joint Dryad-ORCID Symposium on Research Attribution, 23 May 2013

While it seems that digitized scholarship should lead the way to better discoverability and attribution, the age-old problems still plague us: confusion about names, siloed data, and a variety of cultural and technical obstacles. This meeting, hosted jointly by Dryad and ORCID, provided a venue to discuss these issues and learn about the initiatives underway to address them, with attention to both the cultural and technical usage of persistent identifiers for researchers and their work.
