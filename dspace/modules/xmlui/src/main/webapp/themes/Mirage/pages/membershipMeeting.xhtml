The Dryad Membership Meeting will cap off a series of separate but related events spotlighting trends in scholarly communication and research data.

The Now and Future of Data Publishing, a symposium, 22 May 2013

The symposium will provide an overview of the current landscape, interrogate the apparent benefits for researchers and research more generally and examine visions of the future of data publication. Above all, what sort of data publication will most engage researchers and most benefit research? The day will feature keynote presentations and panel sessions addressing key themes around data publication. Register here

Joint Dryad-ORCID Symposium on Research Attribution, 23 May 2013

While it seems that digitized scholarship should lead the way to better discoverability and attribution, the age-old problems still plague us: confusion about names, siloed data, and a variety of cultural and technical obstacles. This meeting, hosted jointly by Dryad and ORCID, provides a venue to discuss these issues and learn about the initiatives underway to address them, with attention to both the cultural and technical usage of persistent identifiers for researchers and their work. Register here

Dryad Membership Meeting, 24 May 2013

Learn about recent developments, get a preview of upcoming features, have a say in the governance of the organization, exchange ideas and experience with other members and Dryad staff, and help chart the future of data archiving. By invitation to Dryad Members, Directors and guests. Register Here
