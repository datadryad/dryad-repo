Integrate your journal 

Dryad frees journals and publishers from the responsibility and costs of publishing and maintaining supplemental data in perpetuity. By encouraging transparency and making possible a broad range of research and educational reuse, journals and publishers increase their impact and reputation.

Journal publishers are encouraged to facilitate data archiving by coordinating the submission of manuscripts with submission of data to Dryad. Dryad communicates in a lightweight and flexible way with most online manuscript processing systems.

Advantages of Submission Integration include:

  • Simplifying the process of data submission for authors.
  • Allowing authors to deposit, to a single repository, gigabytes of data files in their original formats.
  • Reducing the rate of noncompliance with journal data policy.
  • The option of making data available during peer review, via secure access for editors and reviewers.
  • Ensuring bidirectional links between the article and the data prior to publication.
  • Ensuring that the Dryad record can be accessed at the time of online article publication.
  • Giving authors the option to delay release of data for a fixed period after article publication, if consistent with journal policy; editors may authorize custom-length data embargoes.
  • Enhancing visibility of the article via linking from the independently discoverable data files

We welcome Submission Integration for any organization that publishes a scientific journal irrespective of whether the organization is signed up for a payment plan or is a member of Dryad. Please contact director@datadryad.org for more information.
