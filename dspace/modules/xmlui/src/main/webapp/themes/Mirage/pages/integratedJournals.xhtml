Starting 1-Sep-2013, Dryad will introduce a Data Publishing Charge (DPC). The base charge for a single data package is US$80, independent of the number of files, up to a total volume of 10 Gigabytes. There is no charge for rejected submissions to the review workspace; a charge is applied only when the associated publication is already available or in press. In many cases, the base charge will be paid by a sponsoring organization, such as a journal, that has enrolled in a Dryad payment plan. In the absence of a sponsoring organization, the submitter is required to pay the DPC. The submitter may be responsible for additional fees if the publication is not in a journal with integrated submission – see list below – (US$10) and/or the data packages is in excess of 10GB (US$15).
