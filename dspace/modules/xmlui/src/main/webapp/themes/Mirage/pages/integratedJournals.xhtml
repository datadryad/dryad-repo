Starting 1-Sep-2013, Dryad will introduce Data Publishing Charges (DPCs). The table below indicates whether submissions associated with a given journal are covered by a sponsor. In the absence of a sponsor, the submitter pays the DPC of US$80. If the journal is not listed below, the submitter also pays a non-integrated journal fee of US$10. For submissions larger than 10 gigabytes, excess storage fees also apply.
